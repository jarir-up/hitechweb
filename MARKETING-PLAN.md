## Content Guide — Instagram

### Posting frequency
- **3x per week minimum**
- Best times for Karachi B2B: Tue–Thu, 11am–1pm or 7–9pm

### Content pillars (rotate)

| Pillar | Format | Example |
|--------|--------|---------|
| Portfolio | Photo / carousel | "Business card deck we printed for [client type] — 500 qty, gloss laminate" |
| Process | Reel (15–30s) | Offset press running, guillotine cutting, binding in action |
| Social proof | Photo + caption | "Printed the annual prospectus for Cadet College Karampur — 3 day turnaround" |
| Education | Carousel | "Offset vs Digital — which is right for your order?" |
| Offer / seasonal | Story + post | "Eid orders closing [date] — book now to guarantee delivery" |

### Caption formula
```
[Product] for [industry/client type]
[Key detail: quantity · finish · turnaround]

DM or WhatsApp for your quote 👇
wa.me/923343219844
```

## Target Audience Outreach Priority

1. **Corporate SMEs — SITE / Korangi / Nazimabad**
   - Buy: Business cards, letterheads, notepads, envelopes
   - Route: Cold WhatsApp to admin/HR, walk-ins to nearby factories
   - Proof point: 20 years in SITE, same-area delivery

2. **Schools & Institutions**
   - Buy: Prospectuses, registers, diaries, exam stationery
   - Route: WhatsApp to principals/admins in Jul–Aug and Oct–Nov
   - Proof point: Dar e Arqam Schools, Cadet Colleges, Riphah College

3. **Pharma & Textile**
   - Buy: Labels, packaging, product inserts
   - Route: LinkedIn, referrals from existing clients
   - Proof point: Existing pharma/textile portfolio — consistent colour across batches

4. **Event Planners & Wedding Industry**
   - Buy: Invitations, programmes, menus
   - Route: Instagram DMs to Karachi wedding planners and decorators
   - Proof point: Wedding Cards portfolio item

5. **Gift & Packaging Brands**
   - Buy: Custom boxes, labels, ribbon cards
   - Route: Instagram + WhatsApp
   - Proof point: Gift Labs client

## What NOT to Do

- Do not run paid ads (Facebook/Instagram) until Google Business and organic Instagram are consistent — ads need a working funnel first
- Do not compete on price with digital print shops — lead with quality, trust, and turnaround
- Do not post stock images — always use real work from the press
- Do not mention the specific press model anywhere on public channels

## Tracking

Keep a simple WhatsApp note or Google Sheet with:
- New leads this week (source: Google / Instagram / WhatsApp / referral / walk-in)
- Quotes sent vs orders closed
- Top-performing Instagram posts (saves + DMs)
- Reviews collected (target: 10 in first 30 days)
